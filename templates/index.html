Our Objectives

Objective I

To preach unity in the body of Christ by organizing joint meetings and participating in individual members church activities

Objective II

To care for the welfare of pastors and church ministers through humanitarian intervention.

Objective III

To be the voice of the church in Kakamega County, give direction and enforce integrity in the body of Christ by uniting pastors ,fellowships, groupings and network.

Objective IV

To strengthen the church by organizing seminars, leadership conferences, crusades, prayer seminars and encourage commitment to individual church activities..
